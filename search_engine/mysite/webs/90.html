Research Status As a principal investigator or a key technical personnel, I have taken part in multiple NSF, 863, or 973 projects. I investigated the online scaling of RAID, found the reordering window characteristic in scaling a RAID volume. As a consequence, I proposed a series of efficient approaches to scaling RAID volumes, which improved the RAID scalability. I have published 8 papers in journals or conferences. Among of them, one was published in ACM Transactions on Storage, and two were published in IEEE Transactions on Computers.
