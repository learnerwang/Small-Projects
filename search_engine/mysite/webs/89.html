Research Status After graduation in 2007, I worked with Prof. Andrew B. Kahng as a postdoctoral scholar in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, UC San Diego. In my postdoctoral research, I focused on the areas of design for manufacturing, delay and leakage optimization, etc. Collaboratively, we published several papers, including one DAC 2008 paper and one ICCAD 2008 paper (Best Paper Nomination). We also filed a patent in UCSD. Besides, two journal papers have been accepted by IEEE Trans. on CAD of Integrated Circuits and Systems . My current research group focuses on the area of VLSI physical design, including topics of floor-planning, placement, routing, clock tree synthesis and routing, timing analysis and optimization, etc. Honors And Awards ICCAD 2006, 2008: Best Paper Nomination (2006, 2008); CCF (China Computer Federation) Outstanding Ph.D. Thesis Nomination (2007); Tsinghua University Outstanding Ph.D. Thesis Award, Second Class (2007).
